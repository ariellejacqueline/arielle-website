About the Artist

Arielle was born and raised in the San Fransisco Bay Area. She likes to do illustrations and paintings of landscapes. Arielle has been interested in art from a young age and has developed her skills during her time in high school.

To see more of Arielle's art or to get in touch, follow @arielle.jacqueline on Instagram.
